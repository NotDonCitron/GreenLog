# Closed Beta Tester Instructions

## Who Can Test

- 18+ only.
- Invited testers only.
- Do not share the app publicly yet.

## What To Test

- Age Gate
- Account creation and login, if available
- Dashboard
- Grow documentation
- Strains page
- Profile
- Community
- PWA install
- Mobile layout

## What Not To Do

- Do not share screenshots publicly.
- Do not enter real sensitive data yet.
- Do not treat the app as a sales platform.
- Do not treat the app as a medical platform.

## How To Report Feedback

Please include:

- Device
- Browser
- Route or page
- What happened
- Expected result
- Screenshot, if safe

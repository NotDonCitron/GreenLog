# Legal Publication Blockers

## Current Public Launch Status

Public launch, social traffic, and public promotion are blocked until real Impressum data is provided and published.

The app may continue toward a closed internal beta with limited, invited, informed testers. Closed beta testers must understand that the app is not yet cleared for public sharing or public promotion.

## Required Missing Impressum Data

- Legal operator name
- Street and house number
- Postal code and city
- Contact email
- Responsible person, if applicable
- VAT, tax, or business details, if applicable

## Rules

- Do not fabricate legal data.
- Do not use placeholder Impressum data for public or social launch.
- Do not remove this blocker from launch documentation until the real operator data has been provided, published, and verified.
- Keep public/social launch blocked until `/impressum` is complete and has passed a legal-page smoke retest.
